About Me

After graduating with Masters in Civil Engineering (Water Resources Engineering) at the prestigious university of the Maharaja Sayajirao University of Baroda in 2019, I developed interest in GIS during the research project on rainfall-runoff model of a river basin in India. While pursuing graduate certificate in GIS at Fleming College (Canada), I had the opportunity to gain the real-world experience in the GIS industries. After enhancing my GIS technical skills in database development, spatial analysis, cartography, programming, and web mapping solutions, I am looking forward to integrating them with the software technology to provide powerful and innovative solutions for optimizing the water resources to the world.
